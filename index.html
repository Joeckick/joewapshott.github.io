About

I'm a product leader who believes the best PMs can build, not just spec. At Cuvva, I lead a team of five while also serving as PM for a product team — still in the weeds, still shipping.

I've spent a decade in product, moving from enterprise (Atos) through consultancy (TAB) into fintech startups. I've been Head of Product twice — first at Nude through its acquisition by Tembo, now at Cuvva where we've hit 1m customers and profitability.

I've started making small PRs with Cursor because I think speed of shipping is what separates good product teams from great ones. The PM role is bifurcating — those who embrace AI tools and develop real product sense will thrive.

Outside work, I live in the Chilterns with my wife and two young children. I play padel competitively and ran two marathons in 2025.
